Official Community Guidelines


Discord's Community Guidelines are designed to ensure a safe and welcoming environment for all users. Here are some key points from the guidelines:

No Harassment: Users must not engage in any form of harassment, including bullying, sexual harassment, or server raiding.

Respect Others: All members should treat each other with respect and kindness, regardless of personal feelings.

No Inappropriate Content: Sharing pornographic, adult, or other NSFW material is strictly prohibited.

No Spam: Users should avoid spamming messages or disrupting conversations.

Follow Discord's Terms of Service: All users must adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Be Respectful: Encourage members to treat each other with respect and civility.

No Inappropriate Language: Limit the use of profanity and prohibit derogatory language.

No Self-Promotion: Avoid allowing members to promote their own content unless it's in designated channels.

No Offensive Names or Profile Pictures: Enforce rules against inappropriate usernames and avatars.
